MINNEAPOLIS vs ST.PAUL

The land of 10,000 lakes is more than a frozen permafrost!

It is a travel destination that draws the ire of it's neighbors, mostly Wisconsin who looks on across the Hudson River with envy. At the heart of Minnesota are the powerhouse cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul, nestled along the banks of the mighty Mississippi River within a stones-throw of each other. Sure, people come here for the lakes, the north shore, and America's largest mall... but if they're being honest they'll tell you they're really here to sample our world-class culinary offerings. Which prompts us to ask...

Which city reigns supreme when it comes to having best burger?
